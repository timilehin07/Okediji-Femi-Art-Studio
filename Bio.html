My Bio

Hello, I'm Okediji Femi, I’m a visual artist (Sculptor) from Nigeria. I communicate my thoughts, feelings, and ideas about the society where I find myself through my art and also give back to the society by training young students on how to be better in the field of Art. I design and decorate interiors and exteriors with luxury art.
EDUCATION: Yaba College of Technology, Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria.
SKILL: Interior Design, Sculptor at Okediji Femi Art studio
