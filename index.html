I’m a multidisciplinary Artist from Nigeria, who specializes on sculpture. I tell stories through my Art because I want to be a voice to the voiceless who don’t know how to express themselves.

More About Me

To me, Art is a living part of me and I try as much as possible to nurture it everyday. I create new ideas and concept through the happenings around me. The name of my studio is OKEDIJI FEMI ART STUDIO here in Nigeria and I’ve been practicing for quite a while now. I create luxury Art and sculptural furnitures. The situations and things happening in my environment inspires me to always create, which is my way of communicating my thoughts, feelings, emotions and ideas to my audience and this keeps my creativity alive.
